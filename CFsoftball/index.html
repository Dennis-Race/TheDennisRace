June 29 - Game 1

The first game of this Round Robin was against Mac's Pizza/Dew Drop. CF started the game out extra slow and just could not recover from it. CF was hitless through the first 3 innings, it was ugly. Even though CF came alive in the fourth inning scoring 5 much needed runs, that was just about all CF did. The game ended in a 6 to 13 loss.

June 29 - Game 2

The next game wasn't much to watch. Again, CF came out slow and ended even slower. CF was up against Charlie Hustle Softball, and judging by the 1 to 15 loss there wasn't much hustle coming from CF. Getting only 6 hits in the game and scoring just 1 run, will never win you a game. Another ugly, forgettable game.

June 29 - Game 3

After the first two losses of the day, CF Softball kept their heads high and their fight alive. CF's first four batters got on base and also scored giving them a 4 to 0 lead to start the game. The game was against Orange Crush, which CF has played before, and just like before the game ended in the walk off win for CF Softball with the score of 9 to 8. Andy Dickerson lead the way going 2 for 3 with a homerun and 3 RBI's.
