Background

We are a bunch of guys from all over the tristate area who enjoy playing softball. The age of our team varies from 20-62. This team continues a tradition of 35 years of softball under the same sponsor, The Carlisle & Finch Company. We have had many additional sponsors throughout the years, but Carlisle & Finch has always been our mainstay.

We have played at numerous parks over the 35 year history, but this year our main league will be at River City Sports Complex in Cincinnati, Ohio. We play tournaments all over Kentucky and Ohio, so you can check our schedule for updates on where we will be playing on certain dates.
